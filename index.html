About Demon Bot

The ultimate security and moderation solution for Discord

Demon Bot is a powerful, all-in-one Discord bot designed to protect your server from raids, nukes, and spam while providing extensive moderation and community engagement tools. With lightning-fast anti-nuke capabilities, it detects and blocks mass channel/role deletions, mass bans, and webhook abuse in real time.

Beyond security, Demon offers a complete suite of features: AutoMod with profanity and spam filters, a versatile ticket system, customizable welcome messages, giveaways, leveling, reaction roles, and much more. Server administrators can fine-tune every aspect with an intuitive command structure and whitelist system.

Whether you're running a small community or a large gaming server, Demon Bot ensures your Discord stays safe, organized, and engaging. Join our support server for assistance or to suggest new features!
